# What is Hum?

Hum is a privacy-first, federated messenger leveraging Matrix for communication and integrating a Lightning wallet for payments directly in your chat.

## Core Principles

* Privacy by default: end-to-end encryption via Matrix with local key storage.
* Open source: MIT-licensed, community-driven, welcoming contributions.
* Practical payments: seamless Bitcoin Lightning support alongside chat.

## What You Can Do Today

Hum is currently still under development.

## Why Matrix?

* Open protocol with mature end-to-end encryption and cross‑client interoperability.
* Federation lets you pick a homeserver (or host your own) and still talk to anyone on the network.
* Rich feature set: rooms, threads, presence, typing, media, and more.

## Why Lightning?

* Instant, low‑fee Bitcoin payments suitable for micro‑transactions.
* Familiar invoice (BOLT11) flows that map well to chat UX.
* Growing ecosystem and mobile‑friendly SDKs.
